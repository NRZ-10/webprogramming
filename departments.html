Departments

MEA Engineering College offers departments including Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical & Computer Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Information Technology, and Mechanical Engineering, alongside a Science & Humanities department for foundational courses. These departments provide both undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech) programs, focusing on strong infrastructure, experienced faculty, and hands-on learning through well-equipped laboratories and workshops.
