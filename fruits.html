FRUITS

Fruits are an important part of a healthy diet because they are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They come in different colors, shapes, and flavors, making them both nutritious and enjoyable to eat. Common fruits like apples, bananas, and oranges are loved by people of all ages. Seasonal fruits such as mangoes, strawberries, and watermelons are eagerly awaited for their freshness and taste. Many fruits are eaten raw, while some are also used to make juices, jams, and desserts. They help in improving digestion, boosting immunity, and keeping the body energetic. Citrus fruits like lemons and oranges are a great source of vitamin C. Eating a variety of fruits daily can prevent many diseases and promote overall health.
