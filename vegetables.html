VEGETABLES

Vegetables are an important part of a healthy diet as they provide essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They come in different types such as leafy, root, stem, and fruit vegetables. Eating a variety of vegetables helps to improve immunity and keeps the body strong. Green leafy vegetables like spinach and cabbage are rich in iron and calcium. Root vegetables such as carrots, beetroot, and potatoes give energy and improve eyesight. Vegetables like cucumber and tomato keep the body hydrated and fresh. They are low in fat and calories, making them ideal for weight management. Fresh vegetables can be eaten raw, cooked, or made into soups and salads.
